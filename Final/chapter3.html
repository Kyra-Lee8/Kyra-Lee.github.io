Using My Voice

I've been on this journey from since I was in the sixth grade. I started to learn more about what my mission, vision and purpose was. The question I had to ask myself was: what was I placed on this Earth to do that I am aware I can do and love to do?

At the age of five, I learned that both of my mother's lungs had collapsed. This event triggered something in me, as my dad always says, and I have made it my life's mission to make whatever I do in life have a positive effect on others. This deifning moment for me is how I found my voice. Programs like 4-H, helped me to understand how I can use my voice to make a larger impact. #KyraSpeaks #PublicSpeaker

I started 4-H, the Largest global youth development organization, when I was in the sixth grade. I learned about different engineering disciplines,the importance of positively impacting the lives of youth and what it meant to give back to my community. #KyraLuhDaKids #CivicEngagement #STEMMActivist #engineering

At the age of 15 years old, I was named the Youngest Person to ever be appointed to a Community Board in the U.S. From earning this prestigious honor, I realized that my voice was being heard and my passion for giving back to my community becoming more known. #PublicSpeaking #KyraSpeaks
