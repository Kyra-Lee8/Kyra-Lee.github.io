About the Author

My name is Kyra-Lee.
I am a dedicated, passionate and persistent youth leader
with over 9 years of experience in leadership, public speaking and activism to name a few.
This has led to the creation of numerous events for over 900 youth across the globe.
This "book" is dedicated to anyone who is still on this journey called 'life'.
It can be challenging sometimes trying to find your passions and figure out life in general.
Hopefully, my story can serve as encouragement to never give up!
Once what you do comes from your heart, it will show.
